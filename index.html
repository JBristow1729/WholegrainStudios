Hi, I'm Jake, and I make games I want to play

Wholegrain Studios started one afternoon when my friend begged me to make a specific game that we couldn't find an adequate version of online. Lovingly naming it Lavern Dice (The font made "T" look like "L"), we played, tested and iterated until it evolved into my first game - Pips.

I want to make games that are fun to play, not products in disguise trying to steal your money or your attention. Everything in the bakery has been made because I loved doing it.

I hope you enjoy my home-baked games as much as I do!
