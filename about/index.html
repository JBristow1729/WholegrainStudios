What is Wholegrain Studios?

Wholegrain Studios is a tiny, one-person bakery for games and apps. It started one afternoon when a friend begged me to make a specific game we couldn't find an adequate version of online. We played, tested and iterated until it rose into my very first title — Pips.

Since then it's grown into a small shelf of things I genuinely wanted to exist: games that are fun for their own sake, and little apps that quietly make everyday life easier.

Who I am

Hi, I'm Jake. I'm a developer who makes the things I want to play and use. No big studio, no investors leaning over my shoulder — just me, a keyboard, and a stubborn belief that software should feel wholesome.

What I do

I design, build, and bake everything end to end. Every title is made with care — no microtransactions, no dark patterns, no bloat. Just good ingredients and no fillers.

I hope you enjoy my home-baked creations as much as I do!
